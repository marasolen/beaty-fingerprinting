Beaty Biodiversity Museum: Fingerprints

Each glyph represents a subset of the museum. The first glyph is for the entire museum, and the following glyphs are for the individual rows within the museum. The green represents images, the orange represents objects, and the grey represents the rest of the museum (not including that row). The shape is a radar chart without axes, with the angle corresponding to taxonomic class, and the distance from the center being logarithimcally related to the number of items in the subset of the museum that are within that angle's taxonomic class.
